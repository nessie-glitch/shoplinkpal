ShopLinkPal Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 4, 2025

Data Collection

ShopLinkPal is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle data in our Chrome extension:

  • ShopLinkPal stores referral links and coupon codes locally in your browser using Chrome storage.
  • No personal data (e.g., name, email) is collected or shared unless you opt into future shared features (e.g., syncing across devices).
  • We do not track your browsing history, location, or other personal information beyond what you voluntarily enter (e.g., referral details).

Data Usage

Data is used solely to enable the extension’s functionality (saving, sharing, alerting).

Local storage ensures your referrals stay private to your device unless you enable sharing in future updates.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal data to outside parties.

Future shared features (e.g., syncing referrals) will require your consent and a separate privacy notice.

Your Rights

You can clear local storage via Chrome settings (Extensions → ShopLinkPal → Clear Data).

Ensure referral links and coupon codes comply with their providers’ terms (e.g., Amazon, Uber).

Support Link
